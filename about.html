The Janak Foods Story

Rooted in Amritsar. Growing for your table.

A New Standard for Daily Essentials

Established in 2026, Janak Foods was born out of a simple, quiet observation in the heart of Amritsar: the way we shop for our daily needs had lost its soul. We wanted to bring back the feeling of the local market—the reliability of fresh produce, the transparency of knowing where your food comes from, and the ease of modern service.

The Mission

"We believe that fresh, healthy food isn't a luxury—it's a necessity that should be accessible, transparent, and effortlessly delivered to your door."
